President's Intro

EXCESS is one of the non-political student societies of IOE, Purwanchal Campus, Dharan established in 2070 B.S. with a pure and selfless motive to provide a platform for students to showcase their ideas on current technology through various events and competitions organized throughout the semester. Since its establishment date EXCESS have been conducting various training programs, seminars, workshop on various technology-related topics in and outside the campus.

Xech Studio

XTECH Studio is an yearly event featuring number of worshops, seminars, competitions, and mini-events. It is something we're excited about throughout the year.

Basic Microcontroller Training

Protyping with microcontroller should in the repertoire of all you tech creatives out there! We go from programming basic arduino to the handling some magical modules. You'll for sure some out an electronic wiz.

Idea Presentation Competition

We know you've got some game-changing ideas. How about sharing it with us? No, How about competing with it?

We also know you've got more than that, the ability to impact. And this can be the platform.

IOT Training

Do you know there are already 50 billion devices on internet? And the numbers are gonna go up exponentially.

And we want to train you so that you can be a part of this revolution.
